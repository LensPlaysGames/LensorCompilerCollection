* Simple Member Default Initialization

#+NAME: source
#+begin_src glint :tangle ""
  t_foo : struct {
    x : int;
  };

  foo : t_foo;

  foo.x;
#+end_src

Integers are zero-initialized, by default, even when they are the member of a struct.
Structs are also zero-initialized.
